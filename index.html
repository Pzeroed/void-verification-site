Vocabulary / Definitions

  • Constructive Criticism: Feedback intended to improve behavior/content, delivered respectfully.
  • Harassment: Repeated, targeted, or deliberate behavior intended to annoy, intimidate, or harm a member.
  • Spam: Repeated messages, images, links, or emojis that disrupt conversation flow.
  • Minor / Moderate / Severe Offense: See Law Classes.
  • Spoiler: Information revealing plot/outcome; must be tagged.
  • Role Abuse: Misusing roles or permissions to bypass rules.
  • NSFW: Any content not safe for teens; strictly forbidden.
  • Loophole: Attempts to avoid enforcement by exploiting vague language.
